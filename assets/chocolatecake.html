Rich Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water

For the Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Prepare: Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add Wet Ingredients: Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix well with an electric mixer on medium speed.
  4. Add Hot Water: Stir in hot water. The batter will be very thin.
  5. Bake: Pour into prepared pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Make Frosting: Melt butter, stir in cocoa. Add powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla. Beat until spreadable.
  7. Frost: Once cake is completely cool, frost between layers and outside of cake.

Pro Tips

  • Use room temperature eggs for better mixing
  • Don't overbake - check with a toothpick at 30 minutes
  • Let layers cool completely before frosting
  • Use hot coffee instead of water for richer flavor
