Fresh Strawberry Cake

‚è∞ Prep Time: 35 mins üî• Cook Time: 40 mins üçΩÔ∏è Servings: 12 üìä Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 3 cups cake flour
  • 2 cups fresh strawberry puree
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Strawberry Frosting:

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup strawberry puree
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Fresh strawberries for decoration

Instructions

  1. Prepare: Preheat oven to 350¬∞F. Line three 8-inch cake pans.
  2. Make Puree: Blend fresh strawberries until smooth.
  3. Mix Dry: Combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Cream: Beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  5. Combine: Add eggs one at a time, then alternate dry ingredients and puree.
  6. Bake: Divide batter between pans and bake for 35-40 minutes.
  7. Frost: Once cool, layer with strawberry frosting and decorate.

Pro Tips

  • Use real strawberries for the best flavor
  • Don't overmix the batter
  • Let layers cool completely before frosting
  • Keep cake refrigerated
