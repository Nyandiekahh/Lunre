Mixed Berry Delight Cake

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 1/2 cups cake flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup butter, softened
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, raspberries, blueberries)

For the Berry Frosting:

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup berry puree
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Fresh berries for decoration

Instructions

  1. Prepare: Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Grease two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. Mix: Combine flour, sugar, and baking powder. Add eggs, milk, butter, and vanilla.
  3. Add Berries: Gently fold in mixed berries.
  4. Bake: Pour batter into pans and bake for 40-45 minutes.
  5. Make Frosting: Beat butter, gradually add sugar and berry puree.
  6. Assemble: Layer cake with frosting and decorate with fresh berries.

Pro Tips

  • Coat berries in flour to prevent sinking
  • Use room temperature ingredients
  • Don't overmix the batter
  • Chill the cake before frosting
