Classic New York Cheesecake

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt

For the Filling:

  • 4 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust: Preheat oven to 325Â°F (165Â°C). Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Make the Filling: Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, beating on low speed. Mix in heavy cream and vanilla.
  3. Bake: Pour filling over crust. Bake for 1 hour until center is almost set. Allow to cool, then refrigerate overnight.
  4. Serve: Run a knife around the edge of the pan to loosen the cake. Remove the springform pan sides before serving.

Pro Tips

  • Ensure all ingredients are at room temperature before starting
  • Don't overmix after adding the eggs to prevent cracks
  • Use a water bath for even baking and to prevent cracks
  • Cool the cheesecake gradually to prevent sinking
